# Functional Requirements Verification for VLA Module

## Executive Summary

This document verifies that all functional requirements (FR-001 through FR-009) specified for the Vision-Language-Action (VLA) module have been satisfied. The verification assesses whether the implemented content meets the original functional requirements for Module 4 of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Course.

## 1. Verification Methodology

The functional requirements verification was conducted by:
- Mapping each requirement to specific content elements in the VLA module
- Assessing completeness and accuracy of requirement satisfaction
- Validating that requirements are met at the appropriate level of detail
- Confirming that educational context is maintained throughout
